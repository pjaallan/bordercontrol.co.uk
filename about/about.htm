About Border Control

Border Control is a small company and we understand the need for a friendly understanding voice on the end if the phone, we really are only a call away for any support issues or if you would just like a chat about the service we offer.

Border Control can provide Microsoft Exchange Hosted email filtering for the smallest of companies to the largest Enterprise. We can provide direct SMTP email transfer or POP3 collection.

Border Control used to own and maintain its own email spam and virus scanners but now use Microsoft Exchange Hosted email filtering service for its clients. This enables our customers to receive the full benefit of the worlds largest software companies resources.

We have experience with Microsoft's Exchange server 5.5, 2000, 2003, 2007, 2010 and Exchange 2013. We also have extensive experience with Microsoft Small Business Server (SBS)

 

Service Level Agreements

Exchange Hosted Filtering includes comprehensive Service Level Agreements (SLAs) that include:

  • Network uptime: 99.999%
  • Email delivery: Average delivery commitment of less than two minutes
  • Virus Blocking: 100% protection against all known email viruses
  • Spam Capture: Capture of at least 95% of all inbound spam emails
  • False Positive Ratio: False positive commitment of less than 1 in 250,000 emails
